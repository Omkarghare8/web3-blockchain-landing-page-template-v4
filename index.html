Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about NexusChain.

NexusChain is a next-generation blockchain platform designed for DeFi, NFTs, and enterprise applications. It uses a unique consensus mechanism that enables fast transactions, low fees, and high security while remaining fully decentralized.

NXC tokens are available on major decentralized exchanges including Uniswap and PancakeSwap. You can also purchase them through our official platform using ETH, BNB, or stablecoins.

Stakers can earn up to 12% APY on their NXC holdings. Rewards are distributed daily and can be claimed at any time. Longer lock-up periods provide higher rewards.
